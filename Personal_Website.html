About Me

My name is Anup Parajuli. I come from Nepal. I am a fourth-year student at Soka University of America pursuing Life Sciences. My academic interests lie at the intersection of biochemistry, molecular biology, structural biology, and computational biology. I enjoy research that brings together coding, experimentation, and quantitative analysis to explore how protein structure influences function and disease.

In my free time, I find joy in simple and creative things: walking by the beach, cooking, playing guitar, traveling, and exploring museums and art exhibits. I also enjoy writing poems, listening to music, and solving puzzles.

Here is my compact CV if that may interest you. Feel free to email me at aparajuli at soka dot edu to chat more.
